I saw that you enjoyed last week’s post! A lot of people read it and that makes me happy. I have to say that this week has been a very special week so far. I am starting to write on Thursday, February 24, 2022, probably a date that will be written in the history books. I don’t know what will happen in the next few days. I definitely have my college entrance exam tomorrow and I hope it goes well. Everything that is happening in the world is not normal. A pandemic, now a war and then who knows. All this weighs down the days. Even we have lived a pseudo-normality until now, I have never felt as light as I did before the pandemic. I carry heaviness in me. I can’t figure out what it’s due to. It makes me feel the weight of things totally on my shoulders and therefore makes me feel even heavier. I feel like the things I have to do in everyday life are completely on me. Slowly it is becoming a “what a struggle to do this”, “what a struggle to do this other”. It’s kind of lost the magic and beauty that doing things used to have. It probably has it now too but I’m just struggling to see it. It will show! I am confident after all.

I’ve been studying like crazy this week but still got to save a few items to my [Pocket](https://getpocket.com/) list. I recommend it, it’s a place where you can save articles you like and not have them disappear in the whirlwind of information that storms our cell phone or pc.
