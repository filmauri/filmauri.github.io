My first Post

Posted on Mar 30, 2021

Hi everyone!

I am writing this page not knowing what will become of this project.

I’m sure something will come to me though. What made me start this blog even though I didn’t really know what would become of it is the book: Start Now, Get Perfect Later by Rob Moore. I listened to the summary of it on 4books, a startup by Marco Montemagno, and it convinced me. The fundamental message is the one expressed in the title. Start now, then time will take its course.

It was said, “Make a decision, and let what needs to happen happen, with the knowledge that you can correct and control it.”

Certainly by starting this blog I’m not deciding something that could change the fate of humanity so I’m pretty comfortable. My initial idea is to post an article every Sunday so I can keep myself active on this front. Maybe the first articles will not be written in the best way or will be the most effective but for sure they will cover interesting things. Ah that’s it! Regarding what I’m going to write about: they will tend to be things I like, that have impressed me and that I want to share with others.

Enjoy staying on my blog and tell your friends about it!
