Grandi cambiamenti in arrivo

Posted on Feb 13, 2021

I've been thinking about what I can do with this site for quite a while now. For a long time I have not been able to get an article out every week as I promised myself. Writing articles like the ones that have come out so far used to take hours. I used to love getting informed about those topics and getting to the heart of the matter. That is something I can no longer do, it takes too much time. I do, however, enjoy putting my head into running a site like this, poking around to figure out how things work. I don't think I'll be able to get back to the posting schedule I maintained for the first few months, that's for sure. I've been looking for a way to keep posting about interesting stuff me while not having a process of hours of research and company.

I would love for this site, filippo.im, to become a journal of sorts. A place where I can share my interests of all kinds, all-around, for real. I'd like to keep a weekly or bi-weekly basis so that I always have something to say but never too much, so that I don't make endless articles that no one would read. We'll talk about everything, ideas, new discoveries (cool apps, news, scientific breakthroughs and new future-proof technologies), projects, tips and in short, anything I think is cool and useful to share.

I hope you will like this idea, in any case I will do so ;)
