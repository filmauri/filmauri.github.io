When in Rome

Posted on Mar 27, 2022

After posting two articles this week I’m a little short of ideas. By the way, is it normal that you don’t find the article “You have to carry the fire” in the Home of the site. In fact it is an article exclusive for newsletter subscribers! If you want to receive it just leave your email, here.

Now that you, dear reader, have subscribed to the newsletter and read the exclusive article you can understand what we will talk about in this article. I’m going to start right off with the sentiment I described in the final part of that article.

This week has been strange as well. I feel like I’m constantly changing, always with my eyes open to new things. The great thing is that I’m amazed at things from every area: school, friends, the (haunted) party, the world. In every corner there is a gem, something that makes me appreciate the time I spend on those certain things. This generates a strange feeling in me. I find myself in these verses by Guido Gozzano that I have decided to make my own.

Who am I? It’s so strange
among so many strange things
a thing with two legs
called pippomauri!
